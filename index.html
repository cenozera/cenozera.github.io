About

The name cenozera is derived from the name of the 3rd Geological era of time called the Cenozoic era. The 1st era Paleozoic era dates from 540 million – 248 million years ago and is characterized by organisms such as insects,fish etc. The 2nd era Mesozoic era dates from 248 million – 65 million years ago and is primarily characterized by dinosaurs. The extinction of dinosaurs 65 million yrs ago marked the beginning of Cenozoic era, the era of “mammals” and it continues into the present.
