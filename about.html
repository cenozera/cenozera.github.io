About

The name cenozera is derived from the name of the 3rd Geological era of time called the Cenozoic era. The 1st era Paleozoic era dates from 540 million – 248 million years ago and is characterized by organisms such as insects,fish etc. The 2nd era Mesozoic era dates from 248 million – 65 million years ago and is primarily characterized by dinosaurs. The extinction of dinosaurs 65 million yrs ago marked the beginning of Cenozoic era, the era of “mammals” and it continues into the present. Humans, meaning we, are the most populous mammals on Earth and thus cenozera is about celebrating diverse human perspectives.A key element of celebration is fun/merriment. In Old Norse the word for fun is gaman from which in middle English is derived the word “game”. Cenozera focuses on indie games,entertainment and applications keeping in line with it’s ideology. Further, there have been random changes in the nature’s pattern since the arrival of man as he has and continues to alter the flow of natural resources , thus creating an impact upon the world.
